# About Me
Junteng Liu is a first-year PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), working with Professor Junxian He in the HKUST NLP Group. His research focuses on natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML), with a particular interest in:
- Large Language Model (LLM) reasoning and reinforcement learning
- Hallucination mitigation in vision-language models (VLM)
- LLM truthfulness and interpretability
